Our Story

From a Small Gathering to a Growing Ministry

Fountain of Life Worship Center – Dandora was founded under the Worldwide Gospel Churches of Kenya (WGCK) banner, a Pentecostal-rooted denomination committed to Spirit-filled worship and community transformation across Kenya.

Under the visionary leadership of Bishop Dr. Eliud Makunyi, the church has grown from a small prayer group in Dandora to a vibrant congregation touching hundreds of lives every week.

We believe in the authority of the Bible, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the necessity of making disciples. Our doors are open to all.

The Word of God

The Bible is our final authority in all matters of faith and practice.

Prayer & Worship

We are a house of prayer — services saturated with Spirit-led praise.

Community & Outreach

We actively serve Dandora through evangelism, charity, and compassion.

Discipleship

We invest in every member to become a mature follower of Christ.

Rev. Stephen Waithaka

Senior Pastor & Church Head

Bishop Dr. Eliud Makunyi is a passionate man of God called to serve the body of Christ in Dandora and beyond. With years of dedicated ministry, he shepherds the congregation with wisdom and unwavering faith.

He is also the founder of Bishop Makunyi Academy, believing education is central to transforming the next generation.

"Faith without works is dead — we preach the Word and serve our community with our hands."

Admissions — How to Enrol

We welcome applications for all levels throughout the year. Join our growing community of learners at Bishop Makunyi Academy!

  • Visit the school office in person at our Dandora campus
  • Bring previous academic records / KCPE/KCSE results (secondary)
  • Birth certificate and immunization card (pre-primary & primary)
  • Admission fee and first term fees on enrollment
  • Interviews for secondary admissions — by appointment
