We also run Bishop S. Kiguru Academy — a Christian school offering quality education from Pre-Primary to Secondary level right here in Dandora.

Our Story

From a Small Gathering to a Growing Ministry

Fountain of Life Worship Center – Dandora was founded under the Worldwide Gospel Churches of Kenya (WGCK) banner, a Pentecostal-rooted denomination committed to Spirit-filled worship and community transformation across Kenya.

Under the visionary leadership of Bishop S. Kiguru, the church has grown from a small prayer group in Dandora to a vibrant congregation touching hundreds of lives every week.

We believe in the authority of the Bible, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the necessity of making disciples. Our doors are open to all.

The Word of God

We welcome applications for all levels throughout the year. Join our growing community of learners at Bishop S. Kiguru Academy!

  • Visit the school office in person at our Dandora campus
  • Bring previous academic records / KCPE/KCSE results (secondary)

  • Interviews for secondary admissions — by appointment
